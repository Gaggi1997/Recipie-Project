French Toast with Mixed Berries with Truvía® Calorie-Free Sweetener

This French toast with mixed berries recipe contains 13% fewer calories* and 50% less sugar* than the full-sugar version. Recipe developed by Live Better America™. Live Better America is brought to you by Minneapolis-based General Mills and is an online source for refreshing new ideas to eat, connect and live better — the simple, fun and encouraging way! LiveBetterAmerica.com brings you all kinds of mouthwatering healthified™ recipes, the straight scoop on topics you're interested in, and helpful tools for a healthier lifestyle. Makes 6 servings.

SERVING SIZE

250gm

CALORIES

400 kcal

INGREDIENTS

  • 1 cup (250mL) fat-free (skim) milk
  • 1⁄2 cup (125mL) fat-free egg product
  • 1 Tbsp (15mL) Truvía® Calorie-Free Sweetener Spoonable
  • 1⁄4 tsp (1mL) salt
  • 1 tsp (5mL) vanilla
  • 1 Tbsp (15mL) butter
  • 6 slices flourless sprouted 100% whole grain bread
  • 11⁄2 cups (375mL) mixed fresh berries sliced if large
  • 6 Tbsp (90mL) frozen (thawed) sugar-free whipped topping
  • Sugar-free maple-flavored syrup, if desired

DIRECTIONS

  1. In medium bowl, beat milk, egg product, Truvía® Calorie-Free Sweetener, salt and vanilla. Pour into shallow dish; set aside
  2. Heat nonstick griddle or nonstick skillet over medium heat or to 350°F (175°C). (To test griddle, sprinkle with a few drops of water. If bubble jump around, heat is just right.). Grease griddle with butter as needed.
  3. Dip bread into egg mixture; coating each side of bread. Place on griddle. Cook 3 to 4 minutes on each side or until golden brown.
  4. Serve each slice with 1⁄4 cup (60mL) berries and 1 tablespoon (15mL) whipped topping. Drizzle with syrup.
